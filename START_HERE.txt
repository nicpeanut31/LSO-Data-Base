MEMBERSHIP WORKFLOW

- Trainee and Probationary profiles remain in their own lists.

ATTENDANCE
- Monthly calendar for rehearsal and activity scheduling.
- Click a date to open an event or create a rehearsal on that date.
- Overall counts and percentages for Present, Late, Absent, and Excused.
- Search/select one member to see total rehearsals and detailed history.
- Print individual, overall, and event-roster reports. The browser print window can save them as PDF.
